SSL renewal: start all over

When it’s time to renew, we’ll need to go through the whole process again, ugh. In order to minimize the chance of someone hacking your site, stealing your private key and to make sure a company that received an SSL still exists, any SSL lives for a limited time.

Thus, when your SSL dies, CA gives you a new one for the same domain. You can also purchase a brand new cert from SSLs.com store and activate it for the same domain, but the benefit of buying from renewal email is that the remaining days will be transferred to your new SSL. The earliest time to renew is 30 days before expiration. Count to 10 and repeat:

  1. Submit your data to CA
  2. Confirm that you control domain
  3. Pass company checks: organisation validation for OV SSL or extended validation for EV SSL
