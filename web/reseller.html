How it works?

Sign up

We are Namecheap.com’s sister company and, therefore, we use the same reseller system. To get started, register at Namecheap or upgrade your existing account to a reseller one. We don’t require any setup fee or contract.

Tire prices

The higher your total receipt per year, the lower the pricing you’ll get. Total receipt is the sum total of all business you’ve done with us or Namecheap regardless of whether you have domains, hosting or SSL.

Integrate

Go truly white–label. There are two primary ways you can automate order placement and certificate activation with Namecheap SSL Reseller program: Through your own website (API access) or WHMCS SSL module.

Test

Sell SSL as a separate product or add to your product bundles. We integrate easily into any system. Play with our sandbox to make sure it works for you. And it’s even easier with WHMCS — just follow the SSL module installation guide.
